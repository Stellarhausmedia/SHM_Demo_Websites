Good to know

Questions, answered.

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?
Not at all. You can book directly through WhatsApp, by phone, or via our Practo profile — new patients are always welcome.
How much does a consultation cost?
A standard consultation is ₹400 and includes a full examination. If treatment is needed, we'll walk you through a clear, fixed-price plan before anything begins.
Will my treatment be painful?
We use modern, minimally-invasive techniques and gentle anaesthesia. Most patients are surprised by how comfortable procedures like root canals and extractions are here.
Do you offer EMI or payment plans?
Yes — no-cost EMI is available on most major treatments, and we accept cashless claims from leading insurers.
What are your clinic hours?
We're open Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM–2:00 PM and 5:00–8:00 PM. Sunday visits are available on request for emergencies.
