About 20/32

A calm, modern dental studio in Egmore.

20/32 Dentistry is a small, friendly practice in the heart of Egmore, Chennai — built around one idea: that good dentistry should feel calm, clear and completely unhurried. Twenty baby teeth or thirty-two adult ones, we look after every smile in your family.

Honest advice, upfront pricing, gentle hands and up-to-date equipment — with evening and weekend slots that fit around school runs and busy work weeks.
