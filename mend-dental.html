Philosophy( 01 )

We don't sell smiles. We end what keeps you up at 3 a.m.

Most dental marketing is about the mirror. Ours is about the night before — the throb you can hear, the ice water test, the jaw you can't unclench. We built MEND for exactly that patient. If it hurts, you're ours.

Our mark is the kintsugi seam: a fracture, repaired and left visible. We don't pretend nothing broke. We fix it properly, honour the repair, and send you back to your life — usually the same day you called.

Cosmetic work is a fine craft. It just isn't our craft. We stay narrow on purpose: emergency dentistry, root canals, surgical extractions, TMJ and sedation. Depth over menu.

Urgent( 03 )

Don't wait it out. Call now if —

  • Facial swelling spreading toward the eye, jaw or neckInfections in this region can escalate quickly.
  • Toothache with fever or feeling generally unwellThat combination is the body asking for help.
  • Bleeding that hasn't stopped after 20 minutes of firm pressureAfter an extraction or an injury.
  • A knocked-out adult toothKeep it in milk, don't scrub it, come within the hour — it can often be saved.
  • Pain or difficulty swallowing or breathingTreat this as an emergency. If it's severe, go to the nearest ER first.
