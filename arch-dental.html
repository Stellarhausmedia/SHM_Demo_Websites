Invis— align®

Four steps, indexed the way dentists index everything — by quadrant. A scan instead of putty. A plan you approve before anything moves. Trays nobody clocks at dinner.

11

Scan

A five-minute iTero® digital scan replaces impression putty. Your entire bite, captured to the micron, while you watch it assemble on screen.

21

Design

Your clinician choreographs every movement in ClinCheck® — tooth by tooth, week by week — and you preview the finished smile before committing to anything.

Align

A series of clear, removable aligners, each worn around 22 hours a day for one to two weeks. Most people will never notice you're mid-treatment. That's the point.

Movement ends; the discipline doesn't. Custom retainers hold the new architecture in place — quietly, for good.

FDI 22 — Questions Asked often, answered plainly

Most adult cases run six to eighteen months depending on complexity. Your ClinCheck® plan shows the projected timeline — and the projected result — before you commit to anything.

You'll feel pressure for the first day or two of each new tray — that's the plan working. It settles quickly, and there are no wires or brackets to abrade anything.

Most mild-to-moderate crowding, spacing and bite cases respond beautifully to aligners. Complex cases are assessed honestly at consultation — if another route serves you better, we'll say so.

It depends on the number of aligners your case needs. You'll receive a single transparent quote after your scan — no surprises mid-treatment, and staged payment plans are available.
