FAQ

Questions,
answered.

Still unsure about something? A quick consult clears most of it up — and the ₹500 is adjusted against your treatment.

Book a ₹500 consult
01 Do you offer EMI & cashless insurance?
Yes. We accept all major cashless health insurers and offer no-cost EMI on braces, aligners and larger treatments, so you can spread the cost over several months. We'll help you sort the paperwork at the studio.
02 Aligners or braces — which is right for me?
It depends on your case, your routine and your budget. Aligners suit most mild-to-moderate cases and adults who'd rather stay discreet; braces handle complex movement and every age. Your consult and 3D scan give you a clear, honest recommendation either way.
03 How long will treatment take?
Most aligner cases run 6–18 months and braces 12–24 months, depending on how much movement is needed. You'll get an estimated timeline at your planning visit, before anything begins.
04 What age should my child first see an orthodontist?
Around age 7 is ideal for a first check — not to start braces, but so we can spot jaw or spacing issues early. Catching them while your child is still growing often means simpler, shorter treatment later.
05 Is it painful? I'm an anxious patient.
Modern orthodontics is gentle. You may feel mild pressure for a day or two after an adjustment — easily managed. We move at your pace, explain each step, and most patients tell us it was far easier than they expected.
06 What happens after my braces or aligners come off?
You'll get custom retainers to hold your new smile in place — this part really matters. We'll show you exactly how and when to wear them, and we're here for check-ins whenever you need.
