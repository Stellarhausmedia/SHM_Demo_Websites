01 When should my child first see a dentist?
Ideally by their first birthday, or within six months of the first tooth appearing. Early visits are short, gentle and mostly about getting comfortable — and they let us catch anything tiny before it ever grows.
02 My child gets anxious — can you help?
Absolutely — it's one of the things we do best. We move at your child's pace, explain everything in friendly terms and never force a treatment. Most nervous little ones settle within a visit or two.
03 Do you see same-day emergencies?
Yes. We hold slots open every day for toothaches, knocks and breakages. Call or WhatsApp us and we'll get you seen as quickly as we can — often within the hour.
04 Do you offer EMI and accept insurance?
We do. Most major insurers are accepted for cashless treatment, and bigger plans like braces or implants can be split into no-cost EMI. We always confirm the cost with you upfront.
05 Can the whole family book together?
That's exactly how Banyan is built. We keep one record per family and can line up back-to-back appointments — kids, parents and grandparents — so it's all sorted in a single trip.
06 What ages do you actually treat?
Every age — from a baby's very first tooth right through to gentle senior care. One practice and one familiar team for your family's whole journey, from 20 milk teeth to 32 adult ones and beyond.
