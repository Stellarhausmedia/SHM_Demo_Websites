Good to know

Questions, answered.

What is 3D scanning and digital smile design?
Instead of uncomfortable putty moulds, we capture your teeth with a quick digital scan. We can then design and show you a preview of your new smile on screen before any treatment starts.
How do I book an appointment?
The fastest way is WhatsApp or a quick call — you'll get an instant confirmation. You can also book through our Practo profile. New patients are always welcome.
Are same-day crowns really done in one visit?
For many cases, yes. We design and mill the crown in-clinic, so you can often walk out the same day with no temporary crown and no second appointment.
Do you accept insurance and EMI?
We accept cashless claims from leading insurers, and no-cost EMI is available on most major treatments. You'll always get a clear estimate up front.
What are your timings?
We're open Monday to Saturday, 9:30 AM–2:00 PM and 5:00–9:00 PM. Sunday appointments are available on request for urgent needs.
