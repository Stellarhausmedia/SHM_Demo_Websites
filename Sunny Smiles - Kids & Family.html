Good to know

Questions, answered.

How do I book an appointment?
The quickest way is WhatsApp or a quick call — no forms needed. You can also drop your number for a callback or book through our Practo profile. New patients always welcome.
Is the first visit really free?
Yes — new patients get a free first consultation and smile check. If treatment is needed, we'll share a clear, fixed-price plan before anything begins.
Are you good with nervous kids (and adults)?
Absolutely — gentle, fear-free care is our speciality. We go at your child's (or your) pace and keep everyone comfortable throughout.
Do you offer EMI or accept insurance?
Yes — no-cost EMI is available on most major treatments, and we accept cashless claims from leading insurers.
What are your timings?
We're open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM–2:00 PM and 5:00–8:00 PM. Emergency Sunday slots are available on request.
