03  The Dentist
Maxillofacial Prosthodontist & Implantologist

Dr. Aditya Rajaram

B.D.S. · M.D.S. (Prosthodontics) · Fellowship in Oral Implantology

Dr. Aditya Rajaram is an internationally trained Prosthodontist and Implantologist with a special interest in full-mouth rehabilitation and aesthetic dentistry. After graduating top of his class, he completed four years of advanced prosthodontic training abroad, earning a distinction as the most outstanding graduating resident, followed by a clinical fellowship in maxillofacial prosthetics and oncologic dentistry at a leading cancer-care hospital.

04  Heritage

From a single room in Madras to a Chennai institution.

Founded by Dr. Lalitha Rajaram in 1976 in a 240-square-foot room on a quiet Madras road, Rajaram Dental has grown over five decades into a 14,000-square-foot practice with sixteen well-appointed operatories in the heart of Alwarpet.

Trusted by generations of families — and by patients who travel from across the globe — the clinic pairs Western technology with Eastern skill in a sparklingly clean, welcoming environment.

“We have gained the trust of generations of patients who are happy to come back, and bring along their friends and family.”
