About the clinic

Dental care you can trust, right in Velachery

Orabel Dental Care is a multi-speciality dental clinic on 100 Feet Bypass Road, near Vijaya Nagar Bus Stand, caring for patients of all ages with a calm, patient-first approach. From preventive check-ups to advanced cosmetic and surgical treatments, every visit is delivered with modern technology and strict safety standards.

  • Safety & sterilisation first
    Rigorous hygiene and infection-control protocols on every visit.
  • On-site imaging & diagnostics
    In-clinic imaging means faster, more accurate diagnosis.
  • Friendly, experienced team
    Gentle care that puts nervous and first-time patients at ease.
  • Convenient session timings
    Morning & evening sessions, Monday to Saturday, to fit your day.
Our treatments

Complete dental care under one roof

A full range of general, cosmetic and specialist treatments for the whole family.

Braces & Aligners

Metal, ceramic and clear-aligner options to straighten and align your teeth.

Root Canal (RCT)

Comfortable, single- or multi-sitting root canal therapy to save damaged teeth.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement that restores your full bite.

Cleaning & Whitening

Professional scaling, polishing and whitening for a fresh, brighter smile.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Veneers, bonding and smile makeovers tailored to your face and goals.

Crowns & Bridges

Zirconia and ceramic crowns, bridges and full prosthetic restorations.

Tooth Extraction

Safe, gentle removal of wisdom and damaged teeth with minimal discomfort.

Emergency Dental

Fast relief for severe toothache, swelling, trauma and broken teeth.

Plus gum care, dentures, pediatric dentistry and over 60 treatments in total.
