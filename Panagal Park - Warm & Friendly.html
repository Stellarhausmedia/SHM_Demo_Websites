What we do

Feel-good dentistry you can count on

From routine check-ups to a full smile makeover, here's how we help — gently, clearly and at your pace.

Tooth Extractions

Comfortable, careful removals when a tooth can't be saved — with gentle aftercare guidance.

Dental Cleanings

Thorough, refreshing cleanings and exams to keep your teeth and gums healthy for life.

Dental Fillings

Natural, tooth-coloured fillings that restore decayed teeth and blend right in.

Dental X-rays

Modern digital X-rays — up to 90% less radiation, and far more detail for safer diagnosis.

Invisalign Clear Aligners

Straighten your smile discreetly with comfortable, removable clear aligners.

Porcelain Veneers

Custom, natural-looking veneers to refine shape, colour and symmetry — beautifully.
