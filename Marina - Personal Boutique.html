Good to know

Questions, answered.

How do I book an appointment?
The quickest way is WhatsApp or a quick call — no forms needed. You can also book through our Practo profile. New patients are always welcome.
What does a consultation cost?
A standard consultation is ₹400 and includes a full exam. If treatment is needed, we'll explain a clear, fixed-price plan before anything begins.
I'm anxious about the dentist — can you help?
Absolutely. A large part of what we do is gentle, paced care for nervous patients — we'll go at your speed and keep you comfortable throughout.
Do you offer EMI or accept insurance?
Yes — no-cost EMI is available on most major treatments, and we accept cashless claims from leading insurers.
What are your timings?
We're open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM–2:00 PM and 5:00–8:00 PM. Emergency Sunday slots are available on request.
