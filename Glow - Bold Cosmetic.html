Is the free consultation really free?
Yes — this month your first smile consultation with Dr. Reddy is completely free, including a full assessment and a clear treatment plan with pricing. No obligation to proceed.

The fastest way is WhatsApp or a quick call — you'll get an instant confirmation. New patients are always welcome, and evening slots are available.
