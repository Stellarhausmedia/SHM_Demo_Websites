01 — The premise

Precision isn't a luxury in the jaw. It is the difference between an implant that lasts a decade — and one that lasts a lifetime.

Half a millimetre is invisible to the eye. Inside bone, it's the difference between an implant gripping dense structure or sitting near a nerve; between a crown that meets its neighbour perfectly or traps food for years.

Anchor exists for that half millimetre. We are not a general clinic that also places implants — implants and oral surgery are the entire practice, planned and executed with the discipline of an engineering workshop.

04 — Freehand vs guided

Why the guide matters.

Most implant problems are position problems — decided in the second the fixture goes in. We remove that second from the equation.

Conventional — freehand

Judged by eye

  • Position estimated during surgery
  • Flat 2-D X-rays hide depth and nerves
  • Wider incisions, longer healing

07 — Questions

Asked before every consult.

Q—01

Does getting an implant hurt?

Placement happens under local anaesthetic, with optional sedation, so the surgery itself is painless. Most people describe recovery as easier than a difficult extraction — a few days of mild soreness, managed with ordinary painkillers.
Q—02

How long does the whole process take?

First scan to final crown is usually two to four months. Most of that is quiet biology — bone fusing to titanium. Chair time is small: the placement itself takes one to two hours.
Q—03

I was told I don't have enough bone.

That's where we start, not where we stop. Grafting and sinus-lift procedures rebuild lost volume, and the CBCT scan tells us precisely what's possible. A large share of our patients were declined elsewhere first.
Q—04

How long will the implant last?

A well-placed, well-kept implant routinely lasts decades — often a lifetime. The fixture carries a 10-year warranty; daily care and regular reviews protect the rest.
Q—05

What does it cost — and is there EMI?

Single implants from ₹28,000; full-arch from ₹2,40,000 per arch. Your exact quote is fixed after the scan, before anything begins. No-cost EMI runs up to 12 months, with GST-compliant billing for insurance.
Q—06

Will it look like a real tooth?

The crown is custom-milled and colour-matched to its neighbours, then adjusted until the bite feels native. The honest benchmark: most patients stop remembering which tooth it is.
