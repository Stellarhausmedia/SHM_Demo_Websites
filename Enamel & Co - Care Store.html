Do you offer EMI and cashless insurance?
Yes — no-cost EMI is available on treatments above ₹10,000, and we help process cashless claims with 25+ insurers including Star Health, HDFC ERGO and ICICI Lombard. Bring your policy details and we'll handle the paperwork at the desk.
Are the ₹ prices on the site final?
The prices shown are honest starting points — most routine treatments are exactly as listed. Anything complex, like implants or a full makeover, is confirmed with a written quote after your consult, before any work begins.
Do you see children?
Absolutely. Our kids' check-up is built to be gentle and unhurried, and the team is experienced with anxious little ones. Our Family Care Plan covers everyone under one roof.
Is booking really just on WhatsApp?
Add the treatments you're interested in to your enquiry, hit send, and your details open straight in WhatsApp. We reply with the next available slots — no call centre, no holding the line. You can also call us directly any time.
How do you handle sterilisation and safety?
Every chair follows hospital-grade protocols: sealed, single-use kits wherever possible, autoclaved instruments, and surfaces sanitised between every patient. Ask us for a walkthrough whenever you visit.
Do you take dental emergencies?
Yes. Message us with "emergency" and a quick note about the issue, and we'll prioritise a same-day slot wherever possible. For severe pain or swelling after hours, call the clinic line.
