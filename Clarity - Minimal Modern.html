Good to know

Questions, answered.

How do I book an appointment?
The quickest way is WhatsApp or a quick call — you'll get an instant confirmation. You can also book through our Practo profile. New patients always welcome.
Will I know the price before treatment?
Always. A consultation is ₹400 and includes a full exam, and you'll get a clear, itemised, fixed-price plan before anything begins — with no-cost EMI options.
Do you have same-day or evening slots?
Yes — we hold same-day and evening appointments daily, and you can see live availability when you message us to book.
Do you accept insurance?
We accept cashless claims from leading insurers, and no-cost EMI is available on most major treatments.
What are your timings?
We're open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM–2:00 PM and 5:00–9:00 PM. Emergency Sunday slots are available on request.
